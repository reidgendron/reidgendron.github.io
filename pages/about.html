What is a Data Scientist?

"A data scientist is a professional who is responsible for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting complex data using various statistical and machine learning techniques, with the aim of gaining insights that can inform business decisions and strategies. They work with stakeholders to identify business problems, develop hypotheses, design experiments, and analyze data to test those hypotheses. They are also responsible for communicating their findings in a clear and concise manner to stakeholders across the organization, as well as designing and building data-driven solutions that can automate processes, optimize performance, and improve the customer experience. In order to perform their role effectively, data scientists must have a strong foundation in statistical theory, programming, and data manipulation, as well as excellent communication and problem-solving skills." — ChatGPT
